Who are we?

Agri'futur® is an ambitious start-up created by young French students whose ideal is to get closer to nature in a world that has become too industrial. Our simple technologies and green products have also been developed to raise awareness among young people about the benefits of a nature that must be protected without going against the technological projects of the present... and the future!
